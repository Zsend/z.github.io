About Us

The Open Source Prison Education Initiative is dedicated to providing education, wellness, and re-entry programs to incarcerated individuals. Our mission is to create a world where every individual, regardless of their background, has access to the resources they need to succeed.

How We Help

Educational Resources

We provide free courses, reading materials, and workshops to help inmates continue their education and prepare for life after incarceration.

Wellness Programs

Our wellness programs focus on mental and physical health, offering resources to help individuals thrive while incarcerated.

Re-entry Assistance

We help prepare individuals for successful re-entry into society, with career training, job placement support, and community building.
